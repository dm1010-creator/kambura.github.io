Step-by-Step Guide to Grafting Your Avocado

Step 1: Select or grow your rootstock.

Step 2: Select your scion. Ensure it is of similar diameter to the rootstock.

Step 3: Remove the apical growing point of the rootstock with clean pruning shears.

Step 4: Use a grafting knife to make a 1-inch deep cut in the rootstock.

Step 5: Slice the base of the scion diagonally (about 30Â°). Insert it into the rootstock cut and secure with tape or parafilm.

This method ensures consistent fruit production and high-quality avocado harvests.
