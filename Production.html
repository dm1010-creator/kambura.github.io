The Foundation of Great Avocados

At Kamburas Avocados, our success begins with **Site Selection**. Avocados are a subtropical plant that thrive in specific conditions. We carefully select our orchards to ensure a warm climate with minimal frost and well-drained, aerated soil. This meticulous process ensures our trees have the best possible start.
