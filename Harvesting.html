The Final Step to Freshness

Our **Harvesting** process is executed with great precision. Avocados are picked at their ideal maturity for their intended market. For distant consumers, we harvest them when mature but not yet ripe, which allows them to finish the ripening process during transit. This careful picking process is essential to prevent mechanical damage and ensure the fruit arrives in perfect condition.
